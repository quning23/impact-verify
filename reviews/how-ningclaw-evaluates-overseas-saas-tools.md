# How NingClaw Evaluates Overseas SaaS Tools

We evaluate SaaS tools from the point of view of Chinese-speaking founders and small teams.

## Our lens
- workflow fit
- onboarding friction
- pricing realism
- data export and lock-in risk
- support quality
- localization effort
- whether the tool is worth keeping after the trial period

## Our process
1. Read the official product materials.
2. Identify the real user job.
3. Map the tool to a weekly workflow.
4. Note risks, limits, and alternatives.
5. Publish a clear recommendation with disclosure.

## What we value
We prefer tools that help a small team move faster without creating hidden complexity.

## What we avoid
- hype-first reviews
- fake screenshots
- traffic or revenue claims we cannot verify
- affiliate-first pages that hide the real tradeoffs
