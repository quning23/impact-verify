# GetResponse Email Automation Workflow for Small Businesses

Email automation is useful when the same message should not be written by hand every time.

## Good use cases
- welcome sequences
- lead nurturing
- abandoned cart follow-up
- simple newsletter segmentation
- product launch reminders

## Workflow fit
For a small business, the main question is not “does it have automation?” but “can I build a simple flow without turning it into an admin burden?”

## Evaluation checklist
- Can non-technical operators set it up?
- Are the templates useful or cluttered?
- Is list management clear?
- Does the pricing match your subscriber count?
- Is deliverability good enough for your audience?

## Editorial note
We recommend judging the tool by the first three automations you will actually ship.
