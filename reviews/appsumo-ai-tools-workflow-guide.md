# AppSumo AI Tools Workflow Guide for Solo Operators

AppSumo is useful when you want to test a new tool quickly, but the deal page alone is not enough.

## Best fit
- solo founders evaluating lifetime deals
- small teams comparing tools before a larger subscription
- operators who need to map a deal to a real workflow

## What to check before buying
1. Does the tool solve a real recurring workflow?
2. Can you export data if you leave later?
3. Is support active enough for your use case?
4. Are the limits realistic for your volume?
5. Is the discount actually better than the risk?

## A simple buyer rule
If you cannot describe the weekly workflow in one paragraph, do not buy just because the price looks low.

## Red flags
- vague feature lists
- no export story
- unclear support channel
- missing integrations you already need
- marketing language that hides product limits
