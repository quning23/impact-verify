# Frase Content Workflow for Chinese-Language SEO Teams

Frase is most valuable when it is part of a content workflow, not when it is treated as an isolated AI writing tool.

## Good use cases
- briefing long-form articles
- collecting SERP questions
- structuring outlines from search intent
- speeding up research for content teams

## Practical workflow
1. Pick one target query.
2. Review search intent and common questions.
3. Build an outline around the buyer problem.
4. Draft with your own examples and verification notes.
5. Edit for clarity, accuracy, and local audience fit.

## What to watch
- output still needs human editing
- strong prompts matter more than tool magic
- Chinese-language localization needs manual review

## Who should care
Frase fits small teams that need repeatable SEO workflows more than one-off AI drafting.
