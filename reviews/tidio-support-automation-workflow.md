# Tidio Support Automation Workflow for Small SaaS Teams

Support automation should reduce repetitive work without hiding the human handoff.

## Best fit
- small SaaS teams
- storefronts with repeated pre-sales questions
- support desks that need simple routing and fast replies

## What to evaluate
1. Can the chatbot answer the top repetitive questions?
2. Is the handoff to a human easy?
3. Can you keep the tone consistent with your brand?
4. Does it work on the channels you actually use?
5. Are the reporting features simple enough to act on?

## Risk notes
A support bot is not a replacement for good docs, good routing, or a real support owner.
