# Contact

For editorial inquiries, corrections, partnership questions, or disclosure requests, contact the site owner through the public channel listed on the live site.

## What to send
- correction requests
- product update notes
- disclosure questions
- collaboration proposals

## What we do not accept
- spam
- fake sponsorship requests
- requests to hide affiliate disclosure
- requests to publish unverifiable claims

If a direct email address is published on the live site, it should be monitored and kept current.
